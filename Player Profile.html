Player Profile

Rugby is my passion.

My name is Victor Mataele, a Tongan-American collegiate rugby athlete currently living in Provo, Utah. I was born on 27 July 2002 and play in the D1A College Rugby Association of America. I have experience across the backline but my main positions are Fly-half, Inside Center, Outside Center, and Full-back. I’m a backline player who works hard to contribute wherever I’m needed.

I started playing rugby at age 5 in Tonga, where I had the opportunity to represent Tonga U15 as a Scrum-half and Tonga U16 as a Fly-half before moving to the United States in 2018. Those early experiences were meaningful to me and helped shape my understanding of the game, but I still consider myself a player who is growing and improving every season.

My goal is to continue developing my skills, strengthen my decision-making, and be someone my coaches and teammates can rely on. I want to continue contributing at the collegiate level and work toward whatever opportunities come next through consistent effort, humility, and a willingness to learn.
